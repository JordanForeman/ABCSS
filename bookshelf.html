The Bookshelf

I personally recommend each and every one of these books. They are the best of the best at enhancing your knowledge in the areas that they concern.

Don't Make Me Think

by Steve Krug

This book is practically the user-experience bible. Any web designer worth their salt should at least have heard of this book if they haven't already read it twice.

buy on Amazon →

Designing With Web Standards

by Jeffrey Zeldman

Jeffrey Zeldman knows web standards - he coined the term! Designing With Web Standards is a must have in any web worker's library. The book will teach you how to create web pages that are both past and future proof (or at least as much as possible), as well as understand the history of the web and why standards are such an important factor in modern development.
